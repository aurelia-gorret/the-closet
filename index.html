THE CLOSET's Concept

THE CLOSET has been created in 2018 after a simple contastation: typical fast-fashion brands are not paying any attention to their own impact on the planet's destruction. The concept of THE CLOSET is to propose to our customer to rent their outfits for a period of time instead of buying new piece that people only wear 2 or 3 times. Make some savings and act for the environment so that Fashion becomes responsible...
